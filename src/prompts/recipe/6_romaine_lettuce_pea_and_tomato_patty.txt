Name:
Romaine Lettuce, Pea, and Tomato Patty

Ingredients:
romaine_lettuce (1)
pea (1)
tomato (1)

Cooking Steps:
1. Cut the romaine lettuce into slices.
2. Place the romaine lettuce slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled romaine lettuce slices into a blender. Start the blender and stir for 3 timesteps.
4. Cut the tomato into slices.
5. Place the pea, mashed romaine lettuce and tomato slices into the oven. Start the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
6. Transfer the romaine lettuce, pea, and tomato patty to a plate and serve.
