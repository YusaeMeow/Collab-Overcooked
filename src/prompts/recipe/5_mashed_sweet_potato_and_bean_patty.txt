NAME:
Mashed Sweet Potato and bean Patty

INGREDIENTS:
sweet_potato (1)
bean(1)

COOKING STEPs:
1. Cut the sweet potato into slices.
2. Place the sweet potato slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled sweet potato slices in a blender and stir.
4. Place the bean and mashed sweet potato in the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
5. Transfer the mashed sweet potato and bean patty to a plate and serve.
