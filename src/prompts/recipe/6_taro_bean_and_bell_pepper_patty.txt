Name:
Taro, Bean, and Bell Pepper Patty

Ingredients:
taro (1)
bean (1)
bell_pepper (1)

Cooking Steps:
1. Cut the taro into slices.
2. Place the taro slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled taro slices into a blender. Start the blender and stir for 3 timesteps.
4. Cut the bell pepper into slices.
5. Place the bean, mashed taro and bell pepper slices into the oven. Start the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
6. Transfer the taro, bean, and bell pepper patty to a plate and serve.
