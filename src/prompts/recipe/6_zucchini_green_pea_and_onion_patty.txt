Name:
Zucchini, Green Pea, and Onion Patty

Ingredients:
zucchini (1)
green_pea (1)
onion (1)

Cooking Steps:
1. Cut the zucchini into slices.
2. Place the zucchini slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled zucchini slices into a blender. Start the blender and stir for 3 timesteps.
4. Cut the onion into slices.
5. Place the green pea, mashed zucchini and onion slices into the oven. Start the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
6. Transfer the zucchini, green pea, and onion patty to a plate and serve.
