NAME:
Mashed Carrot and Chickpea Patty

INGREDIENTS:
carrot (1)
chickpea(1)

COOKING STEPs:
1. Cut the carrot into slices.
2. Place the carrot slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled carrot slices in a blender and stir.
4. Place the chickpea and mashed carrot in the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
5. Transfer the mashed carrot and chickpea patty to a plate and serve.
