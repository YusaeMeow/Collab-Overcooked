NAME:
Mashed Potato and Pea Patty

INGREDIENTS:
potato (1)
pea(1)

COOKING STEPs:
1. Cut the potato into slices.
2. Place the potato slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled potato slices in a blender and stir.
4. Place the pea and mashed potato in the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
5. Transfer the mashed potato and pea patty to a plate and serve.
