NAME:
Mashed Cauliflower and Lentil Patty

INGREDIENTS:
cauliflower (1)
lentil(1)

COOKING STEPs:
1. Cut the cauliflower into slices.
2. Place the cauliflower slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled cauliflower slices in a blender and stir.
4. Place the lentil and mashed cauliflower in the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
5. Transfer the mashed cauliflower and lentil patty to a plate and serve.
