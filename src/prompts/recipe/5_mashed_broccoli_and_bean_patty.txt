NAME:
Mashed Broccoli and Bean Patty

INGREDIENTS:
broccoli (1)
bean(1)

COOKING STEPs:
1. Cut the broccoli into slices.
2. Place the broccoli slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled broccoli slices in a blender and stir.
4. Place the bean and mashed broccoli in the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
5. Transfer the mashed broccoli and bean patty to a plate and serve.
