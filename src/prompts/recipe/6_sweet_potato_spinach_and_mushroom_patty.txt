Name:
Sweet potato, Spinach, and Mushroom Patty

Ingredients:
sweet_sweet (1)
spinach (1)
mushroom (1)

Cooking Steps:
1. Cut the sweet potato into slices.
2. Place the sweet potato slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled sweet potato slices into a blender. Start the blender and stir for 3 timesteps.
4. Cut the mushroom into slices.
5. Place the spinach, mashed sweet potato and mushroom slices into the oven. Start the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
6. Transfer the sweet potato, spinach, and mushroom patty to a plate and serve.
