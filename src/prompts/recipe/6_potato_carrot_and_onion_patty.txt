Name:
Potato, Carrot, and Onion Patty

Ingredients:
potato (1)
carrot (1)
onion (1)

Cooking Steps:
1. Cut the potato into slices.
2. Place the potato slices in a pot to cook for 3 timesteps.
3. Put the boiled potato slices into a blender. Start the blender and stir for 3 timesteps.
4. Cut the onion into slices.
5. Place the carrot, mashed potato and onion slices into the oven. Start the oven and bake for 5 timesteps.
6. Transfer the potato, carrot, and onion patty to a plate and serve.
