NAME:
Baked Bell Pepper Soup

INGREDIENTS:
bell_pepper(1)

COOKING STEPs:
1. Cut a bell pepper into slices.
2. Place the bell pepper slices in the oven and bake for 3 timesteps.
3. Transfer the baked bell pepper slices to a pot and cook for 3 timesteps.
4. Fill a dish with the soup from the pot and serve.
